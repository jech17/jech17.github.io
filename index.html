LEGACIES

League of Legacies aims to celebrate the greatest moments in League of Legends history. It strives to document famous (or infamous) professional player highlights and provide details about their legacies. With the ever evolving landscape of the game, this site provides a glimpse into the past where legendary moments create the foundation of today's strategies. Let us all look back and honor the achievements and endeavors of these legends!
