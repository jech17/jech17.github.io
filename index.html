The Artifacts of League of Legends
ABOUT

Summoner’s Rift isn’t just a map. It’s our basketball court, our MMA octagon. It’s a place where relationships are forged, where players refine their game over years, and where the best in the world become legends. In a game with endless character matchups and ever-evolving strategy, SR was League’s only constant for five years. We were hesitant to tinker with such a vital piece of the League puzzle, but we felt excited to take on the challenge.
