Brandon "Saintvicious" DiMarco

Brandon “Saintvicious” DiMarco is a veteran professional player known for his famous moments of missing smite objectives at the most crucial times. He has missed so many smites on the professional stage that whenever a player does the same they are immediately referred to as Saintvicious.

Smitevicious originated from this running gag and has evolved into a legacy left behind by Brandon DiMarco.

Brandon "Saintvicious" DiMarco

Bradon DiMarco was an active professional player in NALCS until the 2015 season. He is regarded as a veteran player and has since transitioned into a coaching role for Team Dignitas. He was selected to participate in the 2013 AllStar's Tournament and is known for popularizing unconventional junglers and his unpredictive playstyle.
