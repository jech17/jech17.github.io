Cpt Jack Cleanse
The Frame Perfect Skill
Kang "Cpt Jack" Hyung-woo

Kang “Cpt Jack” Hyung-woo is a former professional LCK AD Carry player known for is exceptional plays on Ashe and Vayne. He was awarded the Best AD Carry Award at the 2012 Korean eSports Awards for his mechanics and ability to carry games to victory. The Cpt Jack Cleanse is performed by perfectly timing a Cleanse to negate Crowd Control effects. This technique requires sufficiently low ping and a vast knowledge on projectile timings.
