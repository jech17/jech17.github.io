The Frame Perfect Skill

Kang “Cpt Jack” Hyung-woo is a former professional LCK AD Carry player known for is exceptional plays on Ashe and Vayne. He was awarded the Best AD Carry Award at the 2012 Korean eSports Awards for his mechanics and ability to carry games to victory.

The Cpt Jack Cleanse is performed by perfectly timing a Cleanse to negate crowd control effects. If done correctly, it will seem as if no crowd control effects were ever applied to the champion.

All professional players strive to master this technique, however, it’s extremely challenging since it requires sufficiently low ping and a vast knowledge on projectile timings. As challenging as it is, it is hugely useful as the frame perfect cleanse can ultimately determine the outcome of a game.
