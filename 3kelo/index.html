The Turnaround Play

It was game 5 in an intense series between Giants Gaming and Team Alternate and Carl “ForellenLord” Lückmann managed to execute one of the most memorable turnaround teamfight plays in professional history.

The 3k elo Shockwave is a tribute to Carl “ForellenLord” Lückmann for his outstanding achievement of being the first player to ever reach an elo of 3000 in solo queue. This technique is done by succesfully landing Orianna’s Shockwave on multiple enemy champions and making a devastating impact towards winning the teamfight. The 3k elo Shockwave is known to be one of the best teamfight turnarounds and perfectly displays why Orianna has been a strong pick through all game metas.

Carl "ForellenLord" Lückmann

Carl Lückmann is the previous mid laner for Team Alternate and was the first player to ever reach 3000 elo in solo queue. In fact, he was also the first to reach any elo above 2700 in the game and thus was nicknamed "The Elo Lord" while the elo system existed. Carl was an active professional player from 2010 and ultimately retired at the end of 2013 to pursue personal endeavors.
